About the vibe of a place

Each city has a different set of characteristics which make it unique. The musical ecosystem is an important dimension of the cultural environment of urban areas.

The attractiviness or wealth of a society can be meassured by the amount of recreational activities they can afford (not just in the monetery aspect) to have. The diversity of these events, will also contribute to enahnce the cultural prosperity of cities. In particular, London has understood that their night activities are one of the fatcts that makes London such a vibrant and exciting place.

Where are these occuring?

For different reasons, certain economic activities tend to locate themselves generating clusters. Certain businesses might benefit from what is known as Economies of agglomeration. In other cases, the city characteristics such as real state, population demographics, or proximity to transport nodes contribute to the creation of natural cluster.

By making the differnt API calls, it becomes possible to map the different gigs and study their spatial distribution. In the following panel, a map of all the gigs gathered during a month was created. Following this, each point was allocated to it's corresponding borough and ward. These results can be found below.

In general, more events were found in the North and East of the city. During this period of time, in Hackney there were 355 events, followed by Islington with 282. Camden and Westminister also showed high level of musical activity.
