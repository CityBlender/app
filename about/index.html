About the vibe of a city

Each city has a set of characteristics that make it unique. One of the ways of exploring this is through the musical ecosystem.

The diversity of these events contributes to and reflects the culture and society of a city.

When should you go out?

Every single city has it's own identity, how does it change throughout the week? Fridays and Saturdays are the busiest days of the week for to live music in London, Mondays the least.

Where do the gigs happen?

Does the location of the gigs reflect the polycentricity of London?

By making the different API calls, it becomes possible to map the gigs and study their spatial distribution. In the following panel, a map of all the gigs gathered during a month was created. Following this, each point was allocated to its corresponding borough and ward. These results can be found below.

In general, more events were found in the North and East of the city. During this period of time, in Hackney there were 355 events, followed by Islington with 282. Camden and Westminister also showed high level of musical activity.
